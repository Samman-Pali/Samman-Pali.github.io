Work

My professional journey is a blend of academic rigour and practical experiences that span across software development, cybersecurity, and customer service/experience. As a Project Manager for the Conference Travel Emissions Tool (CTET) at ANU TechLauncher, I led a team towards creating a sustainable solution for conference planning, emphasizing carbon emission reduction. This experience honed my skills in team management, requirement analysis, and web development using ReactJS.
